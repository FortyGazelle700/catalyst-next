### 1. **What Information We Collect**

We collect:

- **Account Info:** Your name, email, and school (if provided).
- **Content You Share:** Messages, schedules, and groups you create.
- **Usage Data:** How you use Catalyst (e.g., features used, time spent on the app).

### 2. **How We Use Your Information**

We use your info to:

- Help you use Catalyst (like saving your schedules and messages).
- Make Catalyst better by fixing bugs and improving features.
- Keep Catalyst safe by preventing spam and abuse.

### 3. **Who We Share Your Info With**

We **do not** sell your data. We may share info:

- With service providers who help us run Catalyst (like hosting services).
- If required by law (e.g., if a court asks us to share data).

### 4. **How We Keep Your Info Safe**

- We use security measures to protect your data.
- We do our best, but no online service is 100% secure.

### 5. **Your Choices**

- You can update or delete your account info anytime.
- You can contact us at help@bluefla.me if you have privacy concerns.

### 6. **Children’s Privacy**

- Catalyst is for users 13 and older (or with a parent’s permission).
- We don’t knowingly collect data from kids under 13.

### 7. **Changes to This Policy**

- We might update this policy over time.
- If we make big changes, we’ll let you know.

For questions, email us at help@bluefla.me!
